## Joining the community

- To be onboarded to ɖears, you must (i) be a member of the After School community, (ii) come to a ɖears meeting, and (iii) agree to start by offering a service or non-perishable good to the community, to be posted on the listings board. All new members receive 100ɖ upon joining.

## Sending ɖears & “Exit Taxes”

- Members may send ɖears to members or nonmembers (i.e., anyone with an email address). Non-members who hold ɖears may only send them to members.
- Every time you send ɖears, you must note the nature and purpose of the transaction.
- ɖears transfers are untaxed when they are:
  - To members; AND in exchange for:
    - Personal services,
    - Perishable goods,
    - Work on listed ɖears / CAD+SR projects
- ɖears transfers are “exit taxed” when they are:
  - To non-members; OR in exchange for:
    - Non-perishable goods
- Exit taxes are 100% and are payable in ɖears.

## Definitions

- **Personal Services:** Ethereal services that don’t add value to a durable asset.
- **Perishable Goods:** Items intended for immediate consumption.
- **Listed Projects:** Projects listed on the community repo by ɖears members, which contribute to common good. Select CAD+SR / ɖears projects will be listed by the Commons Fund.

## Rule & Role Changes

- Rules may evolve and change over time.
- The ɖears operators and system designers run meetings for voting on rule changes, official selection of Commons Fund projects, and may put their role up for election.

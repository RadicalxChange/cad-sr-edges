## Joining the community

- To join the community, you must (i) be a member of the After School community, (ii) come to a ÐEARS meeting, and (iii) complete the onboarding process (details tbc). You’ll then get onboarded to ÐEARS and receive 100Ð.

## Sending ÐEARS & “Exit Taxes”

- Members may send ÐEARS to members or nonmembers (i.e., anyone with an email address). Non-members who hold ÐEARS may only send them to members.
- Every time you send ÐEARS, you must note the nature and purpose of the transaction.
- ÐEARS transfers are untaxed when they are:
  - To members; AND in exchange for:
    - Personal services,
    - Perishable goods,
    - Work on listed ÐEARS / CAD+SR projects
- ÐEARS transfers are “exit taxed” when they are:
  - To non-members; OR in exchange for:
    - Non-perishable goods,
    - Work on unlisted private / personally-owned projects.
- Exit taxes are 100% and are payable in ÐEARS.

## Definitions

- **Personal Services:** Ethereal services that don’t add value to a durable asset.
- **Perishable Goods:** Items intended for immediate consumption.
- **Listed Projects:** Projects listed on the community repo by ÐEARS members, which contribute to community good and are not privately owned. Select CAD+SR projects will be listed by the Common Pool.
